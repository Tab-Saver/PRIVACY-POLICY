# PRIVACY-POLICY
Privacy Policy for Tab Saver Extension
Last Updated: [11/11/2025]
## Introduction
Tab Saver ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our browser extension.
Information We Collect
1. Web History Data (Stored Locally)
What we collect: URLs, page titles, timestamps, and favicons of browser tabs you choose to save
How we collect it: When you manually save tabs or enable auto-save
Where it's stored: All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local)
External transmission: This data is never transmitted to external servers or third parties
2. Website Content (Opt-In Only)
What we collect: Text content extracted from web pages
How we collect it: Only when you explicitly:
Enable the "Smart Summarize" feature in settings
Opt in to "Allow sending page content to backend API"
Manually click "Summarize" on a specific saved page
Where it's sent: To a backend API (default: https://backend-api-vcjs.onrender.com or your configured URL)
Purpose: To generate AI-powered summaries of saved pages
Default status: This feature is disabled by default and requires explicit user consent
3. Extension Settings
What we collect: Your preferences (auto-save interval, notification settings, etc.)
Where it's stored: Locally in your browser
External transmission: Never transmitted externally
### How We Use Your Information
Local Storage: All saved tab sessions and settings are stored exclusively on your device
Smart Summarize: Page content is sent to a backend API only when you explicitly enable and use this feature
No Analytics: We do not track your browsing behavior, clicks, or general usage patterns
No Personal Information: We do not collect names, emails, addresses, or any personally identifiable information
### Data Sharing and Third Parties
No data sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
Backend API: When using Smart Summarize, page content is sent to a backend service for processing. You can configure your own backend URL in settings
No advertising: We do not use your data for advertising purposes
### Data Security
All data stored locally is protected by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
When data is transmitted for Smart Summarize, it is sent over HTTPS
We do not store or log transmitted content on our servers
### Your Rights and Choices
Access: All your saved data is accessible through the extension interface
Delete: You can delete individual sessions or all sessions at any time
Opt-out: You can disable Smart Summarize and external API access at any time in settings
Local-only mode: The extension works fully without any external data transmission if you don't enable Smart Summarize
### Children's Privacy
Our extension is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
### Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.
